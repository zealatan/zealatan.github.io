👋 About

I am a cellular systems engineer specializing in end-to-end physical-layer system design, from algorithm development to hardware-aware architecture and real-time validation on SDR and RFSoC platforms.

My work focuses on mapping signal processing algorithms to hardware and firmware implementations, optimizing performance-latency-complexity trade-offs, and validating wireless PHY systems through simulation, prototyping, and over-the-air experiments.
